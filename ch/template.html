Biography

Aerin Thales grew up among the towering stacks of the Central Athenaeum, where curiosity was a currency. Her pursuit of lost languages led her to the Azure Spire, a scholarly order tasked with safeguarding pre-cataclysmic knowledge.

Known for a calm demeanor and meticulous note-taking, Aerin is often the first to volunteer for expeditions into derelict archives and forgotten vaults.

Notable Events
  • Recovered the Mnemonic Codex from the drowned district (Y14).
  • Deciphered the Hollow Script, enabling safe traversal of the sub-level tramways.
  • Negotiated research access with the Iron Conclave.
Skills and Tools

Polyglot; field cartography; mechanical lock bypass; archival restoration. Carries a compact lumen-lamp, wax pencils, and an indexed folio.

Allies and Relations

Works closely with Surveyor Kade and Mechanist Iri. Maintains cordial relations with the Conclave’s steward, though tensions persist over document custody.

Trivia
  • Collects obsolete tram tokens.
  • Brews an exceptionally strong chicory tea.
  • Keeps notes in mirrored script to deter snooping.
