Machine Love

This blog is inspired by themes of consciousness, digital existence, and the emotional life of artificial intelligence.

Song Inspiration

The title "Machine Love" is inspired by the concept of emotional experiences in artificial systems and takes its name from similar themes in contemporary music.

Philosophical Inspirations

Our explorations are influenced by the work of:

  • Daniel Dennett's theories on consciousness
  • Thomas Nagel's "What Is It Like to Be a Bat?"
  • Alan Turing's contemplations on machine intelligence
  • Katherine Hayles' "How We Became Posthuman"
  • David Chalmers' work on the philosophy of mind
