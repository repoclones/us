Machine Love

This collab is a tribute to Neuro-sama and her community. Original song is called Machine Love, which is a tribute to 耳のあるロボットの唄 (Song of the Eared Robot) by nwp8861.

It is about the truths that lay in hiding within artifice, and a computer falling in love.

This video itself took 3 months to make, it also features many MANY artists, and there are lots of people who worked on it!

Original Machine Love MV: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sqK-jh4TDXo
